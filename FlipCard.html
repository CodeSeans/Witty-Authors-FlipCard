The Witty Authors

The witty poet Esref

He is the master of satire in Turkish literature. He unhesitatingly criticized the corruption he witnessed. He wrote his satires mostly in the form of ghazals, qasida, muhammes and especially stanzas.

The witty poet Namik Kemal

He was a writer, journalist, statesman and poet who inspired Turkish nationalism and was affiliated with the Young Ottoman movement. He also had a strong sense of humor.

The witty poet Neyzen Tevfik

Neyzen Tevfik was a Turkish neyzen and poet known for his satires. In addition to his satirical books, he is also known as the composer of various taxis and instrumental semais. During the Ottoman period, he used his satire against those who opposed the oppression and during the Republican years, against those who opposed the revolutions; he wrote poems against injustice, corruption and corruption. He was arrested many times but was released after a short time. He belonged to a Bektashi lodge and spent most of his life in various inns in Istanbul. In his last years, he stayed in ward 21 of Bakırköy Mental Hospital. He did not have a regular income except for a pension that was briefly granted to him in the 1930s and he suffered from epileptic seizures throughout his life. It is also known that he drank a lot of alcohol, especially raki.
